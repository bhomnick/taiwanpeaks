About this site

A community resource

Peaks of Taiwan was created in 2020 by Ben Homnick with the goal of becoming an authoritative source of information for English-speaking hikers wishing to explore Taiwan's high mountains. We hope to improve access and safety awareness so more hikers can confidently enjoy Taiwan's natural beauty. This site will always be open-source, community-supported and free to access.

Peaks of Taiwan welcomes contributions from anyone. If you'd like to contribute please choose a section below or contact us.

  • Data and Content

    We welcome any data or content additions to peaks, routes, or informational articles. GPX routes and recommended itineraries are especially useful.

  • Photographs

    Any high-quality photographs of routes or peaks are welcome. Creators will receive attribution for their work. Photographs can be submitted here.

  • Code

    This project is open source and available on GitHub at https://github.com/bhomnick/taiwanpeaks. Please see contribution instructions there.
